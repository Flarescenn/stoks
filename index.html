Beginner

Equity

Equity = Assets - Liabilities

The ownership interest in a company's assets after deducting its liabilities. It represents the residual interest in the assets of a company, also known as shareholders' equity.

P/E Ratio

Evaluates the valuation of a company's stock by comparing its current market price per share to its earnings per share (EPS).

Assets

Assets = Liabilities + Shareholders' Equity

Resources owned or controlled by the company that have economic value and are expected to provide future benefits.
